Heavy Equipment Sales & Rental Businesses – Southeast U.S.

This project visualizes heavy equipment sales and rental businesses across the Southeastern United States. The dataset was compiled from publicly available federal business records and filtered using NAICS industry classifications relevant to heavy equipment markets.

Business addresses were geocoded to generate geographic coordinates and mapped using ArcGIS Online to illustrate the regional distribution of equipment-related businesses.

The analysis focuses on the following states:

  • North Carolina
  • South Carolina
  • Georgia
  • Florida
  • Alabama
  • Tennessee
  • Virginia

This type of spatial visualization can help reveal regional market concentration, identify clusters of equipment-related businesses, and highlight potential areas of opportunity for equipment financing and related services.
